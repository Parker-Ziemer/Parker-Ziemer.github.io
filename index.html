About Parker

Parker Ziemer was born and raised in Fountain Inn, South Carolina. He was homeschooled all his 12k years and worked with his father and brother-in-law in auto-mechanics, odd jobs, and construction.

He currently attends Bob Jones University and is just finishing up his Sophomore year as a B.A. Humanities student. He has signed a contract to work at the Wilds Christian Camp and Conference Center as a lifeguard this summer.

Parker plans on using his degree to specialize into the fields of marketing and advertising, and then pursue real estate after he graduates college. He has already lined up an internship with a residential firm in Greenville, but his true desire it to eventually get into a commercial real estate firm in the greater Greenville area.
